# SolarClean AI

**Predictive Solar Maintenance & Weather Intelligence Platform**

Live demo: https://greentechnology.pages.dev/

## Overview

SolarClean AI is an AI-powered dashboard that helps solar plant operators decide **when to clean their panels** — instead of cleaning on a fixed schedule, it combines weather forecasts, inverter power data, and computer-vision dust detection to recommend cleaning only when it's actually worth the cost and water use.

Includes three demo sites out of the box:
- Sahara Mega Solar Farm (50 MW)
- California Commercial Rooftop (250 kW)
- Bavaria Eco Residential (12 kW)

## Features

### 📊 Smart Solar Dashboard
- Total capacity, peak STC rating, current vs. peak efficiency
- Estimated soiling score (dust buildup) and daily energy loss in kWh
- Predicted next cleaning date and estimated revenue loss per day
- Water conserved by syncing cleaning with rainfall ("Rain Sync")
- 100+ day historical soiling & cleaning trend chart
- Expected vs. actual diurnal power curve

### 🌦️ Weather Intelligence & Rain Forecast Analysis
- Live weather data via the Open-Meteo API (temperature, humidity, wind, rainfall, solar irradiance)
- Rainfall forecast vs. dust accumulation index chart

### ⚡ Power Generation Output Analysis
- Upload inverter CSV telemetry data
- Soiling loss calculated as:
  ```
  Soiling Score = ((Expected Output − Actual Output) / Expected Output) × 100
  ```

### 🤖 AI Predictive Cleaning Recommendations Engine
- Synthesizes 7-day precipitation forecasts, daily inverter degradation, glass abrasion limits, and cleaning ROI to avoid unnecessary washes
- Displays AI confidence score and cleaning payback period

### 🔍 Computer Vision Dust Detection
- Upload a solar panel photo (or pick a sample: clean / slight / moderate / heavy)
- Returns estimated dust coverage %, detection confidence, and an actionable recommendation
- Optional heatmap overlay

### 🌱 Sustainability & ESG ROI Calculator
- Adjustable parameters: array capacity, electricity tariff, manual washing cost, water used per clean
- Outputs net annual cost savings, annual water saved, extra recovered energy, and CO₂ emissions avoided

### 📈 Interactive Performance Analytics
- Monthly energy production, expected vs. actual

### 💬 AI Sustainability Assistant
- Chat assistant that answers questions like "Should I clean my panels today?", "How much energy am I losing?", "Will tomorrow's rain help?", "What is my current efficiency?"

### 🔐 Account System
- Sign in / sign up flow with demo accounts for each of the three sample sites

## Getting Started
1. Visit the [live site](https://greentechnology.pages.dev/)
2. Sign in with one of the quick demo accounts (Sahara 50MW / California 250kW / Bavaria 12kW), or create your own account with your solar capacity
3. Explore the Dashboard, then Weather Analysis, Power Analysis, AI Recommendations, Dust CV Analysis, Sustainability ROI, and Analytics tabs
4. Try uploading your own inverter CSV or panel photo to see the AI analysis in action
